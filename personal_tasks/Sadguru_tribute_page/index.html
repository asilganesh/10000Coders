Sadhguru Jaggi Vasudev

Prominent spiritual leader, yogi, and founder of the Isha Foundation.

Spirituality at his core! - He's a contemporary guru, mystique, yogi and visionary.

Here's a time line of Sadhguru's life:

  • 1957- Sadhguru was born on September 3, 1957, in Mysore, Karnataka, India.
  • 1983 - At the age of 25, he had a spiritual experience that marked the beginning of his inner journey. This experience led him to leave his business and family behind and spend several years traveling across India, practicing intense yogic sadhana (spiritual practices).
  • 1992 - Sadhguru founded the Isha Foundation, a non-profit organization dedicated to promoting well-being and spiritual growth. The foundation is known for its various programs and initiatives, including the Inner Engineering program and the construction of the Dhyanalinga, a consecrated meditation shrine.
  • 2008 - The Isha Yoga Center, a 1500-acre ashram located in the foothills of the Velliangiri Mountains in Tamil Nadu, was established. It has become a hub for various spiritual programs, practices, and community activities.
  • 2017 - Sadhguru's "Rally for Rivers" campaign was launched to raise awareness about the depleting state of India's rivers and the need for their conservation.
  • 2021 - Sadhguru continued to be an influential figure in the spiritual and environmental movements, advocating for sustainability and consciousness.
